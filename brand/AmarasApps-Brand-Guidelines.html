AMARASAPPS · BRAND GUIDELINES
01

The identity, in one page

SUMMARY

AmarasApps is a category-agnostic software studio. Its brand is deliberately not tied to finance or to any single product. Sparepay is the first product and keeps its own navy+gold identity — linked to the studio only by a shared system and a small “by AmarasApps” endorsement.

Personality

Modern & trustworthy. Expressive where it earns attention, grounded everywhere trust is built.

Palette

Aurora, violet-anchored. Spectrum in the mark/hero/sub-brands; deep violet + neutrals for all UI.

Mark

Prism — a faceted “A” refracting the spectrum. One studio → many products.

Typography

SF Pro Rounded 800 wordmark, two-tone “Amaras·Apps”. SF Pro body. SF Mono numerals.

Sub-brands

Each product = the Prism refracting one reserved hue. Sparepay = gold.

Motion

Rich but optional — all gated behind prefers-reduced-motion.

This document supersedes the three exploratory folios (Brand-Folio, Proposal-2, Proposal-3) which remain in /brand as history. Tokens here are mirrored in BRAND.md and assets/css/site.css.

Rules

• Clear space ≥ 25% of mark height.
• Min size 16px (favicon) — split reads via opacity.
• On light grounds keep the tile's dark interior; never place bright facets directly on white.
• Endorsement uses the mono mark, always subordinate to the product wordmark.
